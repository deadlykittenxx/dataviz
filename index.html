Are We Really Okay?

Data Visualization of Student's Mental Health

Understanding the Hidden Struggles

Mental health plays a critical role in academic performance and overall well-being, yet many students suffer in silence. This visualization reveals patterns in student mental health, highlighting the prevalence of depression, anxiety, and panic disorders in relation to key factors such as gender, academic level, year of study, study field, etc. By presenting these insights, we aim to spark awareness and action among students, educators, and researchers.

The Data Behind the Story

Our analysis is based on the "University Students' Mental Health Dataset" from Kaggle, which compiles survey responses from university students in Malaysia.
