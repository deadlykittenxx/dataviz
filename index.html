Are We Really Okay?

Data Visualization of Student's Mental Health

Description

Our visualization explores trends in student mental health, focusing on depression, anxiety and panic disorders in relation to factors such as gender, financial stress, and academic level etc. It is designed for students, educators, and researchers who aim to understand the challenges faced during high level academic pursuits. Mental health plays a crucial role in performance and general health, making it essential to identify key stressors and risk factors. By presenting data-driven insights, we hope to raise awareness and encourage informed actions to support students’ well-being.

Dataset

We use the “University Students' Mental Health Dataset”, sourced from Kaggle. This dataset includes survey responses from university students in Malaysia, capturing information on depression, anxiety, and related factors such as academic background, gender, and financial situation.
